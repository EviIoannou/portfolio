About

Welcome to my "Daily UX Writing Challenges" project. In 15 days, I improved my UX writing skills by tackling real-world prompts from top product organisations. These daily exercises culminated in a final, comprehensive challenge on day 15. Join me as I share the journey and insights gained from this immersive practice, all while emphasising the practicality and hands-on nature of this learning experience. Expanding on my background in technical writing and UX/UI design, I drew upon the course materials of Full UX Writing course from Beginners to Masters on Udemy.com to skillfully interpret the requirements and develop fitting solutions.

Skills and tools

For this project, I focused on developing my UX-writing thinking. When I received a daily challenge, I used Word-type documents to write down the challenge/requirement and my solution. When I had finished this project, I used Figma to outline the requirements, showing my thought process as I created straightforward visual designs to represent my solutions.
