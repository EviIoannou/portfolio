UX/UI design

About

After my studies in frontend development, I was very interested to gain more knowledge and hands-on experience in UX/UI design. I had the opportunity to briefly work for Allihop, an innovative start-up that focuses on making business travelling more eco-friendly. This experience taught me a lot about UX research, UX/UI design, teamwork and working with client requirements.

Personal contribution

During my four-month internship, I collaborated with a senior designer and the development team to design a user-friendly interface. As a UX/UI designer, I played a significant role in crafting smooth and user-centric digital experiences. I worked in Figma to meticulously design user flows that guided users through intuitive interactions. I created multiple screen versions tailored to diverse scenarios, ensuring optimal usability for a range of user journeys. My work was deeply informed by user personas, allowing me to empathise with and address the specific needs of our target audience. Additionally, I contributed to the enhancement of our UI by introducing new icons and integrating fresh features, all in alignment with best practices in user experience design.

An overview of my tasks and achievements:

  1. Creating icons
  2. Preparing lo-fi designs
  3. Transforming lo-fi to hi-fi designs
  4. Taking client requirements and feedback to adjust the design
  5. Creating main components according to Material Design
