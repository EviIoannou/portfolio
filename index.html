Who I am

Designer & developer in Gothenburg

My name is Evi and in 2015 I moved from Greeece to Sweden in order to continue my Master's studies in primary education at Jönköping University. That led me to Gothenburg for my second Master's in IT and Learning, through which I discovered my passion for technology, specifically web design and UX. My studies in Frontend Development at IT-Högskolan in Gothenburg have equiped me with knowledge on many aspects of web development. These include among others HTML, CSS, JavaScript and its frameworks (Vue, React), project management, databases, WordPress, UX and some graphic design.

So far I have worked in two different Swedish companies as an intern. My first internship was for two months at Nejla AB, a consulting company in Gothenburg where I worked with three more frontend developers. Together we worked on creating a new frontend design for an existing platform that is use by administrative staff in different municipalities in Sweden. My personal input included a variety of tasks from UI design to creating complete Vue components. During my second internship (four months), I worked on creating the frontend design for an 3D asset management platform for CharpstAR AB. I collaborated with one more frontend developer and used Vue together with Vuetify to achieve the best, most efficient result. In both cases I learned a lot about teamwork, UI design, project management, frontend development with Vue and even fixing bugs.

In my free time I really like learning new things, like a foreign language or a new skill related to my work. I also love cooking and baking, training or going for a walk, reading and spending time with my family, my husband and my two cats.
