What I do

Technical writing

As a technical writer and UX writer, I craft user-focused content that bridges the gap between technical details and user comprehension. I am focused on creating clear and engaging documentation while ensuring a seamless user experience. I always seek opportunities to apply my skills in producing user-friendly content that enhances product usability.

UX & UI design

I am dedicated to creating exceptional digital experiences that seamlessly blend user-centred design principles with innovative interface solutions. With a deep understanding of user research, information architecture, and interaction design, I transform ideas into intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. I usually work with Figma, but I am even familiar with Adobe XD.

Frontend development

I connect UX requirements and UI design, transforming them into frontend solutions using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Vue.js. My expertise extends to using my knowledge of databases and APIs to optimise the smooth integration between the backend and frontend.

My work

Who I am

UX/UI designer, technical writer and frontend developer in Gothenburg

I'm Evi. During my Master's in IT and Learning at Gothenburg University, I discovered my passion for web design and UX. My studies in Frontend Development at IT-Högskolan equipped me with skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vue, React), project management, databases, WordPress, UX, and basic graphic design. Since then, I have focused on taking my skills in UX research, UI design, and UX/ technical writing to the next level with online courses and personal projects.

I currently work as a technical writer and my main role is to create user-friendly documentation that explains the design and function of vehicles’ mechanical components. I have also worked in three Swedish companies as an intern frontend developer and UX/ UI designer. All my work experiences have taught me valuable skills in teamwork, various types of writing, UX research/ UI design, and frontend development with different tools.

In my free time I really like learning new things, like a foreign language or a new skill related to my work. I also love cooking and baking, training or going for a walk, reading and spending time with my family, my husband and my two cats.
